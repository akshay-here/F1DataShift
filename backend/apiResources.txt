Drivers and Constructors Standings, driver and constructor info, FP, Q, R details - Jolpica API (1950 - present)

driver standings end of the season => https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/driverstandings
constructor standings end of the aseason => https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/constructorstandings

driver standings after x rounds => https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/round/driverstandings
constructor standings after x rounds => https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/round/constructorstandings

Qualifying results only available from 1994 onwards

Driver Stats:

https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/drivers/driverId/results/ => gives all the race results of that driver
https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/drivers/driverId/results/ => gives all the rae results of the driver in that year

https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/drivers/driverId/qualifying/ => gives all the qualifying results of that driver
https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/drivers/driverId/qualifying/ => gives all the qualifying results of the driver in that year

https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/drivers/driverId/sprint/ => gives all the sprint results of that driver
https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/drivers/driverId/sprint/ => gives all the sprint results of the driver in that year

Same for the team stats also

https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/round/drivers/driverId/pitstops/ => gives all the pitstops made by that driver in that year
https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/round/connstructor/constructorId/pitstops/ => gives all the pitstops made by the team in that year and that round

https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/drivers/driverId/driverstandings/ => gives that driver's standings in that year along with wins, points, rounds
https://api.jolpi.ca/ergast/f1/year/constructors/constructorId/constructorstandings/ => gives the team's standings in that year along with wins, points, rounds
